Racetrack game

In my engineering coursework, as my final project, I developed a racetrack game. The way I approached this was by making several pillar structures that acted as points that the car must pass in order to finish a lap. Alternating lights indicated what side of the pillar the car must pass, otherwise a penalty would be incurred. Upon completion of a lap, the total time would be displays for the completion of that lap. During this project, I was tasked with writing all the code and wiring all the hardware.
